Biscoff Milkcake

After a long day of fasting why not treat yourself to something
sweet and delicious that will instantly melt on your tongue.
Here is your easy step-by-step guide to creating the best Biscoff Milkcake.

Servings:6 people

Video Tutorial

Ingredients:

For the Cake:

  • 185g Butter
  • 190ml Vegetable Oil
  • 375g Sugar
  • 2 tsp Vanilla
  • 6 Eggs
  • 375g Self Raising Flour
  • 90ml Milk

For the Milk Mixture

  • 380g Condense Milk
  • 350ml Evaporated Milk
  • 150ml Milk
  • 1tbsp Biscoff spread

For Whipped Cream

  • 600ml Double Cream
  • 150g Icing Sugar
  • 2 tsp Vanilla

For Decoration

  • Biscoff Spread
  • Biscoff Biscuits

Instructions:

  1. Pre-heat your oven to 170c.
  2. Grease and line a 9x12inch cake tin.
  3. Add the butter, oil, sugar and vanilla into a bowl and beat these together on high speed for 5 minutes
  4. Add 3 Eggs and mix until combined. Then add the remaining 3 eggs.
  5. Add half of the flour and mix well.
  6. Then add the milk and the rest of the flour and combine until you have a smooth batter.
  7. Pour the batter into the tin and bake in the oven for 50-60minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
    *note*:once this has baked let it cool completely before continuing.
  8. Next, Mix all the milk ingredients together to create the milk mixture.
  9. Poke holes into the cake and pour the milk mixture over the cake
    *Tip: pour the mixture little at a time allowing the cake to soak it in.
  10. Cover and leave this to soak for at least an hour or overnight.
  11. Add all the whipped cream ingredients into a bowl and whisp together until it thickens and stiff peaks form.
  12. Pipe or Spoon the whipped cream over the cake. Add some crushed biscuits and drizzle with biscoff spread.
  13. Finally, Enjoy!
